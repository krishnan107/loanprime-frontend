FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LoanPrime and how our loan comparison platform works.

What is LoanPrime and how does it work? +

LoanPrime is a loan aggregator platform that connects you with 25+ RBI-registered banks and NBFCs. Simply fill out your details, compare real-time offers from multiple lenders, and apply for the loan that best fits your needs — all in one place, completely free of charge.

Is LoanPrime a direct lender? +

No, LoanPrime is not a direct lender. We act as a comparison and aggregator platform that helps you find the best loan offers from multiple verified lending partners. All loans are disbursed directly by the respective RBI-registered banks or NBFCs.

How quickly can I get my loan approved? +

Most loan applications receive in-principle approval within 5 minutes of submission. Funds are typically disbursed within 24-48 hours of completing the e-KYC process, depending on the lender's process and document verification.

What documents do I need to apply? +

The process is 100% digital and paperless. You only need your PAN card number, mobile number linked to Aadhaar, basic income details, and email address. No physical documents need to be submitted or couriered at any stage.

Does checking my loan eligibility affect my credit score? +

No. Checking your eligibility and comparing loan offers on LoanPrime is a soft inquiry and does not affect your CIBIL or credit score in any way. Your score is only impacted when a lender makes a hard inquiry after you formally apply with them.

Is there any fee for using LoanPrime? +

Absolutely not. LoanPrime is completely free for borrowers. Comparing loans, checking your credit score, and getting matched with lenders — all of it is at zero cost to you. We earn a referral fee from the lender only when you successfully apply and get funded.
